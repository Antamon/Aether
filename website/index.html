The world of Aether resembles our own, yet history here has taken a few unexpected turns...

At the end of the 19th century, humanity spent nearly twenty years trying to annihilate itself. Unimaginable scientific progress in the art of war has turned Europe into a devastated nightmare. Now, a generation later, it is up to you to help rebuild a better future. Our story takes place a hundred years ago, in the university city of Raetsburg, where scholars and visionaries study the lingering consequences of the Great War.

In Aether, you can take on the role of noble, middle-class citizen, or laborer in a society desperately clinging to outdated Victorian values. Each social class comes with its own set of privileges and obligations.

The game has a strong emphasis on role-play and social interaction, where your choices and behavior truly shape the world around you.

Safety

Our events are safe and inclusive. We believe in a community that embraces diversity and rejects any form of discrimination based on gender, ethnicity, faith, sexual orientation, physical ability and any other aspect of people's identity. There is no room for verbally or physically abusive behaviour at our events and anyone behaving inappropriately will be removed.

During the event a Safety Team of experienced, reliable and empathetic people will always be available to offer support and to help you manage your emotional wellbeing and to support you with practical advice on how best to develop your gaming experience.

During the event you will always be able to stop and find a dedicated space in case of particularly intense scenes and emotions that you are struggling to manage.

There will be game mechanics to emotionally and physically protect our participants. In addition, part of the initial workshops will focus specifically on this issue.

Never Again War

That was the sentiment that prevailed across Europe after the Great War. During the Treaty of Athens, the European powers pledged to adhere to common rules. Technological and scientific advancements are internationally moderated by the EBI. Faith has been united under the banner of the Holy Family. International cooperation and trade flourished like never before.

And yet, dark clouds of war are gathering once again on the horizon. The great powers overlooked a player who managed to remain miraculously unnoticed. From the shadows, this new enemy launched a devastating attack, inflicting severe damage on the logistical, military, and economic strength of Western Europe.

The time has come to answer difficult questions. Is this the beginning of a new arms race? Can the delicate balance be preserved, or will everything collapse like a house of cards? And who will emerge as the strongest survivor from this chaos? These and other questions are on everyone's lips across all levels of society. Will this spark be extinguished, or is it the start of a new, all-consuming inferno?
